Frequently Asked Questions

Does Lynaris replace existing video surveillance systems?

No. Lynaris complements your current system.In most cases, it connects directly to your existing cameras to make them intelligent, without any change to your installation.

Do I need to install new hardware?

Only if the current infrastructure does not meet the minimum technical requirements (image quality, stream stability).If needed, Lynaris provides and installs compatible hardware, with no complexity for the client.

Is the solution GDPR compliant?

Yes, by design. Image processing is performed locally, data streams are encrypted, and only strictly necessary data is used.No biometric data is stored or exploited.

Are faces or identities recognized?

No. Lynaris never attempts to identify individuals.The analysis focuses solely on behaviors and interactions with the environment, ensuring full respect for privacy.

How long does installation take?

A standard installation takes about one hour and can often be done remotely.
When an on-site technician is required, the intervention remains quick and without business interruption.

Who receives the alerts?

You freely choose the authorized recipients: manager, security lead or management.Alerts are sent only to contacts defined by the client, on the devices of their choice.

Are the recommendations automatic?

Yes.Recommendations are automatically generated from in-store data.They are clear, practical and immediately usable by teams.

Is it suitable for small businesses?

Absolutely. Lynaris works perfectly with a limited number of cameras and small retail spaces.
The solution is designed to be accessible and profitable, even for small stores.

What is the impact on in-store teams?

Very low. Lynaris reduces passive monitoring and frees up time for higher-value tasks, without adding workload for teams.

Can return on investment be measured?

Yes. Lynaris tracks prevented losses, detected thefts and the impact of applied optimizations.ROI is measurable, clear and monitored over time.
